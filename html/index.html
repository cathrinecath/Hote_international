About

Us

Hote International Pty Ltd is a professional Australian immigration agent authorized by MARA.The company is headquartered in the heart of Sydney, Australia. With years of experience in the industry, the company has strong Australian political and political resources, and is involved in immigration, study abroad, finance, real estate and other related businesses. The company's senior consultant team is familiar with domestic and foreign policies, laws and regulations, education system and various industry characteristics, and maintains long-term friendly and close cooperation with Australian institutions, financial institutions, government departments, accounting firms, law firms and other institutions. Our services include, but not limited of migration services, investment projects services and finance services and so on.
